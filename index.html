Projects
Re-ACT on Sensing: Force-Aware Action Chunking with Responsive Temporal Ensemble for Robot Manipulation
paper / code

Developed a novel, force-aware algorithm that was inspired by the remarkable ability of humans to adapt to physical disturbances. The model uses a temporal ensemble method to intelligently re-weight its planned actions the moment an external force is detected, achieving human-like adaptability in its reaction.

Impedance-Controlled Gait Assistance Robot for children with poliomyelitis

Developed a safe and adaptive gait-assistive robot to improve mobility for children with poliomyelitis, focusing on the mechanical design and the application of impedance control. The implemented control strategy ensures the robot moves compliantly and safely with the user, providing gentle, adaptive support.
